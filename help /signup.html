Signup Help

Username Issues

Q: What are the username requirements?

A: Your username must be between 3-10 characters long and can only contain letters, numbers, and underscores (_).

Q: Why is my username not available?

A: If the username you selected is already in use by another user, you will see a message indicating it's not available. Try a different username.

Password Issues

Q: What are the password requirements?

A: Your password must be at least 6 characters long.

Q: How do I see my password while typing?

A: You can toggle the visibility of your password by clicking the eye icon next to the password field.

Email Validation

Q: Why am I getting an email validation error?

A: Make sure your email is correctly formatted. It should follow this structure: username@example.com. Ensure there are no spaces or missing characters.

Profile Photo Upload

Q: How do I upload my profile photo?

A: After selecting an image, you will have the option to crop it to fit the required aspect ratio. Once cropped, it will be uploaded automatically.

Q: Why is my photo not uploading?

A: Ensure that your photo is a valid image file and that you have cropped the image before submitting. If the problem persists, please try again or contact support.
